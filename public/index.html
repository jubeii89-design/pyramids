Crossword Pyramids

Stack. Steal. Spell to Win!

Enter Room Code

▲ Party Play, Jackbox Style

Host on the big screen. Everyone joins from their phone with a QR code or a 4-letter room code. 2–4 players — add AI opponents to fill seats.

▲ A Crossword in 3D

120 white letter pyramids in Red, Blue, Green & Gold, stacked five high across a 10×10 board. Every peak shows its point value, and the tracker shows every pyramid left in play.

▲ Steal to Win

Your opponents' pyramids are fair game. Every word must use one of your letters and one black house letter — the rest is plunder.

▲ Score the Peaks

Every pyramid you capture scores its peak value. Empty your color from the board to end the game — leftover pyramids count against you!

Please remember to have fun — but most importantly, always spell to win.
